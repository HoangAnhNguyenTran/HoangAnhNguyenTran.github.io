During my internship in the First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) program organized by Amazon Web Services Vietnam Co., Ltd., **from May 5, 2026 to July 30, 2026**, I had the opportunity to experience a professional working environment and apply the theoretical knowledge gained at university to practical cloud computing projects. Under the guidance of my industry mentor, Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung, I strengthened my technical knowledge while developing essential workplace skills.

Throughout the internship, I participated in deploying cloud infrastructure on AWS, configuring networking and security services, building a highly available web application architecture, and collaborating with teammates on workshop-based assignments. These activities enhanced my understanding of cloud architecture, infrastructure management, documentation practices, collaboration, and independent learning. I consistently completed assigned tasks responsibly, respected the internship regulations, and actively sought feedback from mentors and teammates to continuously improve my performance.

## Strengths

- A solid foundation across cloud fundamentals, especially IAM, networking, compute, storage, database, and monitoring.
- Strong discipline in weekly progress tracking, technical note-taking, and evidence organization.
- Better ability to connect theoretical knowledge with practical implementation decisions.
- Improved teamwork through collaborative workshop planning and execution for CloudBrief.
- A receptive mindset toward feedback, with willingness to revise deliverables for higher quality.

## Areas for Improvement

- Increase speed and confidence when diagnosing complex issues across networking, permissions, app logic, and managed services.
- Go deeper into infrastructure automation using Infrastructure as Code for more reliable deployment and change control.
- Improve confidence in presenting technical decisions, especially when explaining security-cost-operations trade-offs.
- Gain more hands-on experience with production-like workflows such as CI/CD, containerization, and integration testing.
- Continue strengthening technical-English reading skills to accelerate research on new services and patterns.

## Post-Internship Direction

After this internship, I plan to continue growing toward Cloud/DevOps and Solution Architecture. In the short term, I will focus on IaC, AWS security practices, and operational observability. In the medium term, I aim to independently design and deliver complete systems with stronger testing, monitoring, and cost-optimization standards. This internship confirmed that this direction aligns well with my major and long-term career goals.
